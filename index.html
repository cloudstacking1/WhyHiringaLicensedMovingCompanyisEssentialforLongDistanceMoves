FAQ's

When choosing a moving company, consider factors such as their reputation, services offered, pricing structure, insurance coverage, and experience with the type of move (local or long distance). Reading customer reviews and asking for recommendations can also help you make an informed decision.

The cost of hiring a moving company depends on factors like the distance, the size of your home, the number of items being moved, and the time of year. Local moves are generally cheaper than long-distance moves, and additional services such as packing or storage can increase the cost.

Reputable moving companies are required to provide insurance for your belongings during transit. This can include basic coverage, which typically covers a portion of the value of your items, or full-value protection, which offers more extensive coverage. Always ask about insurance options before booking a move.
